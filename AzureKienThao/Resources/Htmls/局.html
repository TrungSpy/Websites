キョク/KYOKU/    つぼね/tsubone/KYOKU/キョク/    tsubone/つぼね/

bureau;  board;  office;  affair;  conclusion;  court lady;  lady-in-waiting;  her apartment local;  pièce;  bureau;  affaire;  conclusion;  dame de cour;  dame d'honneur;  son appartement à elle junta;  escritório;  caso;  conclusão;  dama da corte;  amante;  apartamento dela oficina;  departamento

Radical: 尸 (corpse).   Strokes: 7画.   Elements: 口尸.   Pinyin: jú.   Hangul: 국 [gug].

Jōyō Kanji 3rd Grade.   JLPT N3.
