午

common

ゴ/GO/    うま/uma/GO/ゴ/    uma/うま/

noon;  sign of the horse;  11AM-1PM;  seventh sign of Chinese zodiac midi;  signe de la 7ème branche terrestre;  signe du Cheval (zodiaque);  11 h à 13 h;  Sud (180°) meio-dia mediodía

Radical: 十 (ten).   Strokes: 4画.   Elements: ノ干十.   Pinyin: wǔ.   Hangul: 오 [o].

Jōyō Kanji 2nd Grade.   JLPT N5.

▸ Example compounds

Example compounds:
ゴGO端午【たんごtango】Boy's Day celebration (May 5th) (one of the five annual festivals)
子午線【しごせんshigosen】the meridian
正午【しょうごshōgo】noon; mid-day
うまuma午【うまuma】seventh sign of Chinese zodiac (The Horse, 11am-1pm, south, May)
午年【うまどしumadoshi】year of the horse
初午【はつうまhatsūma】first "horse day" in any lunar month, but esp. the second lunar month; traditional day for worship at "fox deity" shrines
