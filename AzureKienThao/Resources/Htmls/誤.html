mistake;  err;  do wrong;  mislead erreur;  faute;  mal faire;  induire en erreur erro;  errar;  fazer errado;  enganar error;  equivocarse

Radical: 言 (speech).   Strokes: 14画.   Elements: 言口ハ.   Pinyin: wù.   Hangul: 오 [o].
