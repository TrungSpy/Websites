服

common

フク/FUKU/FUKU/フク/

clothing;  admit;  obey;  discharge habillement;  admettre;  obéir;  libérer (renvoi);  avaler (médicament) vestuário;  permitir;  obedecer;  descarregar ropa;  admitir;  obedecer

Radical: 月 (moon).   Strokes: 8画.   Elements: 月又卩.   Pinyin: fú / fù.   Hangul: 복 [bog].   Nanori: はっ / はつ / はらha' / hatsu / hara.

Jōyō Kanji 3rd Grade.   JLPT N4.

▸ Example compounds

Example compounds:
フクFUKU服薬【ふくやくfukuyaku】taking medicine
服する【ふくするfukusuru】to obey; to serve (e.g. in the army, or a sentence in jail (gaol)); to observe (e.g. mourning); to drink (tea, medicine, etc.)
平服【へいふくheifuku】ordinary clothes; everyday attire; plain clothes; civilian clothes
