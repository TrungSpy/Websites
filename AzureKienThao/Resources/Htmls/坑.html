坑

common

コウ/KŌ/KOU/コウ/

pit;  hole mine;  fosse;  trou buraco;  furo hoyo;  agujero;  caverna;  mina

Radical: 土 (earth).   Strokes: 7画.   Elements: 土亠几.   Pinyin: kēng.   Hangul: 갱 [gaeng].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
