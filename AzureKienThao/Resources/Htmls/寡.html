寡

common

カ/KA/KA/カ/

widow;  minority;  few très peu;  minorité;  veuve viúva;  minoria;  poucos poco;  solo;  viudo

Radical: 宀 (roof).   Strokes: 14画.   Elements: 一自刀ハ宀.   Pinyin: guǎ.   Hangul: 과 [gwa].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
