sociable;  kind;  courteous;  hospitable;  cordial sociable;  gentil;  courtois;  hospitalier;  cordial sociável;  espécie;  cordial;  hospitaleira;  cortês sociable;  amable;  cortés;  hospitalario;  cordial

Radical: 心 (heart).   Strokes: 17画.   Elements: 艮心爪犭豸.   Pinyin: kěn.   Hangul: 간 [gan].
