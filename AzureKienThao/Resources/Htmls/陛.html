陛

common

ヘイ/HEI/HEI/ヘイ/

highness;  steps (of throne) son altesse;  marches (du trône) alteza;  degraus (do trono) alteza;  emperador;  emperatriz;  escalera de palacio

Radical: 阜 (mound).   Strokes: 10画.   Elements: 土比⻖.   Pinyin: bì.   Hangul: 폐 [pye].

Jōyō Kanji 6th Grade.   JLPT N1.
