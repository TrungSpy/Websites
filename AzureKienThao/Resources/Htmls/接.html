セツ/SETSU/    ショウ/SHŌ/    つ.ぐ/tsu.gu/SETSU/セツ/    SHOU/ショウ/    tsu.gu/つ.ぐ/

touch;  contact;  adjoin;  piece together joindre;  toucher;  contact;  recoller (morceaux) tocar;  contato;  juntar;  pedir juntamente acercarse;  aproximarse;  unir;  conectar;  entretener;  dar la bienvenida;  contactar

Radical: 扌 (hand).   Strokes: 11画.   Elements: 女立扌.   Pinyin: jiē.   Hangul: 접 [jeob].
