侯

common

コウ/KŌ/KOU/コウ/

marquis;  lord;  daimyo marquis;  seigneur;  daimyo marques;  senhor;  daimyo marqués;  señor feudal;  daimyo

Radical: 亻 (human).   Strokes: 9画.   Elements: ⺅矢.   Variants: 矦.   Pinyin: hóu / hòu.   Hangul: 후 [hu].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
