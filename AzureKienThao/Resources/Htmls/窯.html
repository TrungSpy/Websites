窯

common

ヨウ/YŌ/    かま/kama/YOU/ヨウ/    kama/かま/

kiln;  oven;  furnace four;  four à poterie;  fournaise fornalha;  forno horno

Radical: 穴 (cave).   Strokes: 15画.   Elements: 王穴并羊儿宀灬.   Variants: 窰窑.   Pinyin: yáo.   Hangul: 요 [yo].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
