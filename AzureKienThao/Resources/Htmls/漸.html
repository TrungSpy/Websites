ゼン/ZEN/    やや/yaya/ZEN/ゼン/    yaya/やや/

steadily;  gradually advancing;  finally;  barely progressivement;  en fin de compte;  finalement;  tout juste firmemente;  gradualmente avançando;  finalmente;  raramente gradualmente;  firmemente

Radical: 水 (water).   Strokes: 14画.   Elements: 斤車氵.   Pinyin: jiàn / jiān.   Hangul: 점 [jeom] 참 [cham].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
