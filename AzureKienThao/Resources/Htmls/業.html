業

common

ギョウ/GYŌ/    ゴウ/GŌ/    わざ/waza/GYOU/ギョウ/    GOU/ゴウ/    waza/わざ/

business;  vocation;  arts;  performance activité;  vocation;  arts;  représentation;  karma negócios;  vocação;  artes;  performance negocio;  industria;  trabajo

Radical: 木 (tree).   Strokes: 13画.   Elements: ｜一王并木羊耒.   Pinyin: yè.   Hangul: 업 [eob].   Nanori: なり / のぶnari / nobu.

Jōyō Kanji 3rd Grade.   JLPT N4.
