僧

common

ソウ/SŌ/SOU/ソウ/

Buddhist priest;  monk prêtre bouddhiste;  moine Monge budista monje budista;  bonzo

Radical: 亻 (human).   Strokes: 13画.   Elements: ⺅田日并.   Pinyin: sēng.   Hangul: 승 [seung].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
