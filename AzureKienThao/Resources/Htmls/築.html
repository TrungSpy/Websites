チク/CHIKU/    きず.く/kizu.ku/CHIKU/チク/    kizu.ku/きず.く/

fabricate;  build;  construct bâtir;  construire;  fabriquer fabricar;  construção;  construir edificar;  construir

Radical: 竹 (bamboo).   Strokes: 16画.   Elements: 工竹木丶几.   Pinyin: zhú / zhù.   Hangul: 축 [chug].   Nanori: つい / つき / つく / づきtsui / tsuki / tsuku / duki.

Jōyō Kanji 5th Grade.   JLPT N2.
