ホウ/HŌ/    たな/tana/    -だな/-dana/HOU/ホウ/    tana/たな/    -dana/-だな/

shelf;  ledge;  rack;  mount;  mantle;  trellis étagère;  rayon;  rebord;  support;  tablette;  manteau (cheminée);  treillis (vigne) prateleira;  apoio;  moldura;  manto;  grade estante;  repisa

Radical: 木 (tree).   Strokes: 12画.   Elements: 月木.   Pinyin: péng.   Hangul: 붕 [bung] 팽 [paeng].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
