葉

common

ヨウ/YŌ/    は/ha/YOU/ヨウ/    ha/は/

leaf;  plane;  lobe;  needle;  blade;  spear;  counter for flat things;  fragment;  piece feuille;  lobe;  aiguille;  brin (herbe);  fragment;  compteur d'objets plats folha;  avião;  ponta;  agulha;  lâmina;  lança;  sufixo p/ contagem de coisas chatas hoja;  contador de cosas planas;  follaje

Radical: 艹 (grass).   Strokes: 12画.   Elements: 木世⺾.   Pinyin: xié / yè / shè.   Hangul: 엽 [yeob] 섭 [seob].   Nanori: よ / わyo / wa.

Jōyō Kanji 3rd Grade.   JLPT N3.
