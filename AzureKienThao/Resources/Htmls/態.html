タイ/TAI/    わざ.と/waza.to/TAI/タイ/    waza.to/わざ.と/

attitude;  condition;  figure;  appearance;  voice (of verbs) attitude;  condition;  état;  apparence;  forme (d'un verbe) atitude;  condição;  figura;  aparecimento;  voz (de verbos) figura;  apariencia;  actitud;  situación

Radical: 心 (heart).   Strokes: 14画.   Elements: 月心匕厶.   Pinyin: tài.   Hangul: 태 [tae].

Jōyō Kanji 5th Grade.   JLPT N1.
