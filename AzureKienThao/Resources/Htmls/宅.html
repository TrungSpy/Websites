タク/TAKU/TAKU/タク/

home;  house;  residence;  our house;  my husband domicile;  maison;  résidence;  notre maison;  mon mari casa;  lar;  residência;  nossa casa;  meu marido hogar;  casa;  residencia;  marido propio

Radical: 宀 (roof).   Strokes: 6画.   Elements: ノ一乙宀.   Pinyin: zhái / zhè.   Hangul: 택 [taeg] 댁 [daeg].   Nanori: け / たか / たけ / や / やけke / taka / take / ya / yake.

Jōyō Kanji 6th Grade.   JLPT N3.
