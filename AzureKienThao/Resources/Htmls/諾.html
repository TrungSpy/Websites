common

ダク/DAKU/DAKU/ダク/

consent;  assent;  agreement consentement;  assentiment;  accord consentir;  consentimento;  acordo consentimiento;  asentimiento;  acuerdo

Radical: 言 (speech).   Strokes: 15画.   Elements: ノ一言口⺾.   Pinyin: nuò.   Hangul: 낙 [nag] 락 [rag].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
