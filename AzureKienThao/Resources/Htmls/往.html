オウ/Ō/    い.く/i.ku/    いにしえ/inishie/    さき.に/saki.ni/    ゆ.く/yu.ku/OU/オウ/    i.ku/い.く/    inishie/いにしえ/    saki.ni/さき.に/    yu.ku/ゆ.く/

journey;  travel;  chase away;  let go;  going;  before;  formerly aller simple;  aller;  parer (coup);  voyage;  parfois viagem;  caça embora;  deixada ir;  indo ir;  tiempos antiguos;  a veces

Radical: 彳 (step).   Strokes: 8画.   Elements: 王丶彳.   Variants: 徃行.   Pinyin: wǎng / wàng.   Hangul: 왕 [wang].   Nanori: みちmichi.

Jōyō Kanji 5th Grade.   JLPT N1.
