common

フ/FU/    ちち/chichi/FU/フ/    chichi/ちち/

father père pai padre

Radical: 父 (father).   Strokes: 4画.   Pinyin: fù / fǔ.   Hangul: 부 [bu] 보 [bo].

Jōyō Kanji 2nd Grade.   JLPT N5.
