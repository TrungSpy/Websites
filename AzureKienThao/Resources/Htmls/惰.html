惰

common

ダ/DA/DA/ダ/

lazy;  laziness paresse preguiçoso;  ociosidade pereza;  descuido;  vaguear;  descuidar

Radical: 心 (heart).   Strokes: 12画.   Elements: ノ一月工忄.   Variants: 怠.   Pinyin: duò.   Hangul: 타 [ta].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
