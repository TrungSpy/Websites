グン/GUN/    いくさ/ikusa/GUN/グン/    ikusa/いくさ/

army;  force;  troops;  war;  battle armée;  forces;  troupes;  guerriers;  bataille exército;  força;  tropas;  guerra;  batalha ejército;  tropas

Radical: 車 (car).   Strokes: 9画.   Elements: 車冖.   Pinyin: jūn.   Hangul: 군 [gun].

Jōyō Kanji 4th Grade.   JLPT N2.
