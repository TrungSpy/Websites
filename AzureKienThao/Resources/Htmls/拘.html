コウ/KŌ/    かか.わる/kaka.waru/KOU/コウ/    kaka.waru/かか.わる/

arrest;  seize;  concerned;  adhere to;  despite arrestation;  saisir;  adhérer;  être concerné;  malgré prisão;  prender;  interessada;  aderir;  desgosto arrestar;  agarrar;  coger;  adherir;  afectar

Radical: 扌 (hand).   Strokes: 8画.   Elements: 口勹扌.   Pinyin: jū.   Hangul: 구 [gu].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
