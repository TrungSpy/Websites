コツ/KOTSU/    ほね/hone/KOTSU/コツ/    hone/ほね/

skeleton;  bone;  remains;  frame os;  squelette;  restes;  charpente esqueleto;  osso;  permanecer;  moldura hueso;  esqueleto

Radical: 骨 (bone).   Strokes: 10画.   Elements: 月冖冂.   Pinyin: gǔ / gū / gú.   Hangul: 골 [gol].

Jōyō Kanji 6th Grade.   JLPT N2.
