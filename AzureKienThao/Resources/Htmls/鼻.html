鼻

common

ビ/BI/    はな/hana/BI/ビ/    hana/はな/

nose;  snout nez;  museau;  groin nariz;  focinho;  tromba nariz;  hocico

Radical: 鼻 (nose).   Strokes: 14画.   Elements: 自田廾.   Pinyin: bí.   Hangul: 비 [bi].

Jōyō Kanji 3rd Grade.   JLPT N2.
