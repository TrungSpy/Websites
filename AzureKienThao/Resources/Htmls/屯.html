屯

common

トン/TON/TON/トン/

barracks;  police station;  camp garnison;  poste de police;  camp militaire quarteis;  estação de polícia;  acampamento barraca;  campamento;  cuartel de policía

Radical: 屮 (sprout).   Strokes: 4画.   Elements: ノ乙凵.   Pinyin: tún / zhūn.   Hangul: 둔 [dun] 준 [jun].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
