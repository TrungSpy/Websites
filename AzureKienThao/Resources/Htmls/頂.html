チョウ/CHŌ/    いただ.く/itada.ku/    いただき/itadaki/CHOU/チョウ/    itada.ku/いただ.く/    itadaki/いただき/

place on the head;  receive;  top of head;  top;  summit;  peak placer au sommet;  recevoir (je reçois de vous);  sommet de la tête (vertex);  sommet;  pic colocar na cabeça;  receber;  topo da cabeça;  topo;  reunião de cúpula;  pico cima;  cumbre;  colocar sobre;  recibir;  comer;  beber (humilde)

Radical: 頁 (face).   Strokes: 11画.   Elements: 一貝目ハ頁亅.   Pinyin: dǐng.   Hangul: 정 [jeong].

Jōyō Kanji 6th Grade.   JLPT N3.
