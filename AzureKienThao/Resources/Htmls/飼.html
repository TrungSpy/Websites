飼

common

シ/SHI/    か.う/ka.u/SHI/シ/    ka.u/か.う/

domesticate;  raise;  keep;  feed domestiquer;  élever;  garder;  nourrir domesticar;  levantar;  guardar;  alimentar criar;  mantener;  tener animales

Radical: 食 (eat).   Strokes: 13画.   Elements: 口艮亅个食.   Pinyin: sì.   Hangul: 사 [sa].   Nanori: かいkai.

Jōyō Kanji 5th Grade.   JLPT N1.
