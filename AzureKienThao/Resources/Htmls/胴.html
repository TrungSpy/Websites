胴

common

ドウ/DŌ/DOU/ドウ/

trunk;  torso;  hull (ship);  hub of wheel tronc (du corps);  torse;  coque (bateau);  moyeu tronco;  torso;  casco (navio);  cubo de roda tronco (cuerpo);  torso

Radical: 月 (meat).   Strokes: 10画.   Elements: 月口冂一.   Pinyin: dòng.   Hangul: 동 [dong].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
