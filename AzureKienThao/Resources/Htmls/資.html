common

シ/SHI/SHI/シ/

assets;  resources;  capital;  funds;  data;  be conducive to;  contribute to capital (argent);  fonds;  actifs;  ressources;  données;  favorable à;  contribuer à bens;  recursos;  capital;  fundos;  dados;  se levado a;  contribuir para recurso;  capital;  naturaleza;  posición social

Radical: 貝 (shell).   Strokes: 13画.   Elements: 貝目ハ欠冫.   Pinyin: zī.   Hangul: 자 [ja].   Nanori: すけ / もとsuke / moto.

Jōyō Kanji 5th Grade.   JLPT N3.
