殉

common

ジュン/JUN/JUN/ジュン/

martyrdom;  follow by resigning martyre;  se sacrifier;  suivre dans la mort martírio;  sair por pedir demissão martirio;  sacrificio

Radical: 歹 (death).   Strokes: 10画.   Elements: 日勹歹.   Pinyin: xùn.   Hangul: 순 [sun].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N1.
