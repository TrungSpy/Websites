雇

common

コ/KO/    やと.う/yato.u/KO/コ/    yato.u/やと.う/

employ;  hire employer;  embaucher emprego;  alugar emplear;  contratar

Radical: 隹 (small bird).   Strokes: 12画.   Elements: 戸隹一尸.   Pinyin: gù.   Hangul: 고 [go] 호 [ho].

Jōyō Kanji High School.   JLPT N2.
